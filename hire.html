HOW TO COMMISSION WORK

What to expect in the process

STEP ONE

Send us your inspiration pictures, the budget and your best idea of the timeline. It's ok if you see something you like but don't know what it is or how it is made, we can help you figure that out. Give us all the information you can and we begin researching what we can do for you.

STEP TWO

We will contact you as soon as we can within two business days and go over options. Once we have a solid idea of your needs we will prepare a spread of options with prices and timelines. We can also create renderings you can use with your client for a fee. Once you have chosen your best option, a contract is drawn up.

STEP THREE

Once the contract is signed you pay a 50% deposit and work begins.
